# ExpenseTracker — Support

ExpenseTracker is a personal iPhone app for tracking expenses across multiple
currencies and accounts. This page is for help, bug reports, and questions.

## Frequently asked

**Where is my data stored?**
On your iPhone. ExpenseTracker doesn't have a server, doesn't create an
account, and doesn't sync to the cloud in this version. If you delete the app,
your data is gone.

**Does the app work offline?**
Yes — it's fully offline. No internet connection is required.

**Can I export my data?**
Not in this version. Export to CSV is planned.

**How do I change the USD ⇄ LBP rate?**
Settings → Exchange rate. The rate you set applies to every conversion
across the app, and you can override it on individual expenses.

**The "available balance" line on the dashboard isn't showing what I expect.**
By default it counts everything due before the end of the calendar month. If
you get paid on a different day, set Settings → Available balance → Payday
and enter the day of the month you're paid. The line will then count
everything due before your next paycheck.

**A subscription charged itself when I opened the app — is that intended?**
Yes. Subscriptions with a renewal date in the past are auto-logged as
expenses on launch and the renewal date advances to the next cycle. You can
turn this off by marking the subscription Paused in its editor.

## Privacy

ExpenseTracker does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All
financial information stays on your device.
